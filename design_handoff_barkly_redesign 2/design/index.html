The Barkly way

Made the slow way.

Every piece is patterned on a real dog, sewn in batches of forty, and finished by the same small studio that sketches the originals. We use brocades, fairisles, and floral cottons sourced from heritage mills — textiles you'd find in a tailor's archive, scaled to fit a four-legged frame.

"A coat your dog could pass down."
  • Patterned and graded XS through L on real dogs of every shape
  • Quilted cotton lining, brushed for warmth, soft on the coat
  • Adjustable belly straps; no Velcro, no plastic snaps
  • Made in lots of forty in our Bay Area atelier
